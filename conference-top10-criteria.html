What makes a conference great?

This is the maximum that a conference can do, but obviously: The more, the better...

Pre-Conference

  1. Make travel assistance terms clear in the CfP
  2. Ask for important information, such as allergies, food preferences, special needs
  3. Take care of travel and accomodation & keep the speaker posted.
  4. Have an info-email with all information that the speaker will need: Travel & accomodation, airport transport, venue, talk slot, aspect ratio, A/V adapters, after party, speaker dinner, ...
  5. Organise transfer from the airport to the hotel.
