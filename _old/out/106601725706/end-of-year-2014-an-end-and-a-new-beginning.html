End of year 2014 - an end and a new beginning

December 30th 2014

#End of year 2014 - an end and a new beginning

So, I think it’s time to wrap up 2014.

The year has been a blast and I’m very grateful for the people I met and my friends and family for making this year so wonderful.

I’ve started writing an ebook on web components which has sold 208 times so far.

I had the pleasure to be part of Codebits VII, together with my friends and former colleagues Balgan, Rafael and Pedro(https://twitter.com/pfcabrita) - it’s been an honour to join the Security CTF with these guys and we made 2nd place, being 1st just until a few seconds before the end.

I also gave my “Build your own OS for fun and profit” talk in Lisbon and met a bunch of lovely people (e.g. the amazing David and the famous Christian Heilmann.

In May I had the chance to meet the brilliant community and conference-hosting genius Patrick in Barcelona and get to know Sam and Francisco who showed me around the vibrant and beautiful Barcelona.

In Barcelona I had the chance to talk about web components at FutureJS and the MEAN Stack at WeNode where I met a bunch of lovely, smart people.

A week later I was invited to the 2014 edition of The Geek Gathering in Croatia. I spend a lovely day in Zagreb before meeting the folks in Osijek - thanks to Andrej, Dennis and all the others for The Geeks Gathering and thanks in particular to my friends in Osijek over at Lovely HQ, yes, I’m looking at you Mario and Marina :)

Then the next special shoutout goes to the Swiss (JS) developer community and the Frontend Conf crowd. Thanks to Pierre and Joel for making the various meetups in and around Zurich happen that I had the pleasure to attend, meeting nice folks like Steffi, Gregor or Philip among so many others (sorry for not listing you all here)!

I also had the chance to visit a few nice german developer conferences and different crowds at enterJS where I met Sebastian and Frederic.

I also spoke at the Mobile App Europe and the Dev.Talks, both of which was nice and interesting.

I also had a visit from Jan-Erik here in Zurich and met the brilliant Googler Michael over a burger

.

I also left Centralway to continue my ongoing journey to support and foster the open web, its ecosystem and the community. I am very happy to have found the amazing team at [Archilogic] (http://archilogic.com) and that I am now part of the endeavours to push the web in general and WebGL in particular to new frontiers. for instance I got to work with the Oculus Rift and the experimental WebVR API. Amazing stuff coming out soon and I'm happy to be aboard of such a wonderful team!

I also had the opportunity to meet the lovely and welcoming developer community in Amsterdam for the DevFest NL, it's been nice to meet so many lovely folks and it was a lot of fun!

Then, last but not least, this year I got to work with a bunch of great people, some of which are still in Zurich, some of which aren't. Of those who haven't been mentioned yet, I'd like to particularly give a shoutout to my former team lead Ollie and my mentor and team mate Marco as well as the seasoned Tim and Eduardo; I learnt an awful lot from these people.

It has been a great year and I hope (and believe) 2015 will be a great year as well.

On to the next big adventures we shall roam! A happy new year to y'all!
