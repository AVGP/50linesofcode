Test your website design with 5 second tests

January 3rd 2013

#Test your website design with 5 second tests

If you have a mockup or final design and you want to find out, how your potential visitors would react, what they think of it or if they even understand it, you should conduct a 5 second test.

FiveSecondTest.comÂ allows you to upload a screenshot or mockup picture and ask a bunch of questions to the users.

The test is run by showing the image for 5 seconds and presenting the questions to the tester afterwards.

There is a free plan you can leverage by earning Karma points. You can do so by taking tests for others.

As each test only takes 5 seconds, you quickly get a bunch of results.

Be sure to make your questions exact enough. Questions like "Do you like the design" don't work (you'll end up with answers like "yes" or "no" without specification on why)
